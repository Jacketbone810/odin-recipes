Chocolate Chip Cookies

Description

You can never go wrong with a chocolate chip cookie. Basically, it is the default delicious snack to bake. I remember that the reason I started to bake was because it was the only way to get my mom to buy me an infinite amount of sweets. If I just made them, she would finance it, since I was developing a skill. Now, I do it because I love the process and the look on my loved one's faces when they take a bite of something they've asked for. Sometimes, its a simple chocolate chip cookie.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup butter, softened
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • 1 cup packed brwon sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • 2 teaspoons hot water
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 3 cups all-purpose flour
  • 2 cups semi-sweet chocolate chi[s
  • 1 cup chooped walnuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Gather ingredients, make sure butter is softened, and eggs are at room temperature.
  2. Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  3. Beat butter, white sugar, and brwon sugar with an electric mixer in a large bwol until smooth.
  4. Beat in eggs, one at a time, then stir in vanilla.
  5. Dissolve baking soda in hot water. Add to battter along with salt.
  6. Stir in flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  7. Drop spoonfuls of dough 2 inches apart onto ungreased baking sheets.
  8. Bake in preheated oven until edges are nicely browned, about 10 minutes.
  9. Cool on the baking sheets briefly before removing to a wire rack to cool completely.
  10. Store in airtight container or serve immediately and enjoy!
